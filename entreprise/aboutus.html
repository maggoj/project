About Us

As you may already be aware, ladies tend to accumulate a large collection of clothing that sits unused in their closets. In order to address this issue, we have come up with a business strategy for selling used women's clothing. This is incredibly beneficial since it allows individuals to purchase the clothing at reasonable costs while also recycling it.

Customers will be able to use our services both in-person and online, therefore we have developed a website through which individuals may purchase or sell clothing. Our primary objective is to contribute 40% of sales to Breast Cancer Research.
